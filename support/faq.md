# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### 1. Why are prayer times slightly different from my local mosque?
Prayer calculation times depend on the **Calculation Method** (e.g., ISNA, MWL, Diyanet) and **Asr Jurisprudence** (Standard vs Hanafi) selected in the settings. You can switch methods in Settings or use the **Manual Minute Offsets** feature to align times exact to your mosque.

### 2. Why am I not receiving notifications on Android/iOS?
Please verify the following settings on your phone:
- **Notifications Enabled**: Ensure notification permissions are granted for Light in device settings.
- **Battery Optimization / Low Power Mode**: Ensure Light is excluded from aggressive battery saver optimizations so background reminders fire reliably.
- **Do Not Disturb**: Confirm that Do Not Disturb or Focus mode permits alarm/notification alerts from Light.

### 3. Does Light store or collect my location data?
**No.** All prayer time calculations occur locally on your device. Your location coordinates are used exclusively for computing sun angles and are never transmitted to external servers or third parties.

### 4. How do I change the notification sound to full Adhan?
Go to **Settings** > **Notifications** inside the app, select the specific prayer (e.g., Fajr), and choose **Full Adhan** as the alert sound.
