# Privacy Policy

*Last updated: July 23, 2026*

**Light** ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application handles user data.

---

## 1. Data Collection and Usage

**Light is designed with a strict privacy-first architecture:**

- **No Personal Data Collection**: We do not collect, process, store, or transmit personal data such as names, email addresses, phone numbers, or account details.
- **Location Data**: To calculate accurate local prayer timings, Light accesses your device's geographical location (latitude, longitude, and timezone). Location data is **processed exclusively on-device** and is never uploaded, shared, or sent to external servers.
- **No Third-Party Analytics or Tracking**: We do not integrate third-party advertising networks, user tracking SDKs, or data brokers.

---

## 2. On-Device Storage

All app preferences (such as selected calculation methods, custom offsets, notification sound settings, and language choices) are saved locally on your device using encrypted native storage.

---

## 3. Permissions

The app requests the following device permissions solely to provide core functionality:
- **Location Permission**: Required for automatic prayer timing computation.
- **Notification Permission**: Required to schedule and display prayer alerts and play chosen reminder sounds.

---

## 4. Children's Privacy

Light does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

---

## 5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
