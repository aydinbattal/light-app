# Light - Prayer Reminder

**Light** is a modern, accurate, and privacy-focused Islamic prayer time & reminder app for iOS and Android. Built with a sleek dark aesthetic, precise astronomical calculation engines, offline capabilities, and customizable sound notifications (Adhan, Chimes, Beeps).

---

## Highlights

- 🕌 **Accurate Prayer Calculations**: Supports major calculation conventions globally (MWL, ISNA, Egypt, Umm al-Qura, Karachi, Tehran, Gulf, Moonsighting Committee, Kuwait, Qatar, Singapore, Turkey, France, Russia).
- 📍 **GPS & Manual Location**: Automatic location-based prayer timing with timezone offset and high/low latitude adjustments.
- 🔔 **Custom Reminders & Sound Options**: Choose between full/short Adhan, Fajr Adhan, soft chimes, or gentle beeps for each prayer.
- 📅 **Hijri Calendar Integration**: Easily view Islamic calendar dates alongside solar dates.
- 🔒 **100% Privacy Focused**: All calculations are performed on-device. No location data or personal info is ever uploaded or stored externally.
- 🌙 **Modern Dark Interface**: Designed for maximum clarity and battery efficiency.
