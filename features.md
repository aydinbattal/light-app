# Features Overview - Light

**Light** is designed from the ground up to provide Muslims around the world with exact prayer schedules, configurable notifications, and complete peace of mind.

---

## 1. Precise Prayer Engine

- **Global Calculation Methods**:
  - Muslim World League (MWL)
  - Islamic Society of North America (ISNA)
  - Egyptian General Authority of Survey
  - Umm Al-Qura University, Makkah
  - University of Islamic Sciences, Karachi
  - Institute of Geophysics, University of Tehran
  - Gulf Region Standard
  - Moonsighting Committee Worldwide
  - Kuwait & Qatar Standards
  - Majlis Ugama Islam Singapura (MUIS)
  - Presidency of Religious Affairs, Turkey (Diyanet)
  - Union Des Organisations Islamiques De France (UOIF)
  - Spiritual Administration of Muslims of Russia
- **Asr Calculation Methods**: Standard (Shafi, Maliki, Hanbali) and Hanafi jurisprudence options.
- **High Latitude Rule Adjustments**: Middle of the night, Seventh of the night, or Angle-based calculations for Northern/Southern regions.

---

## 2. Smart Reminders & Custom Sounds

- **Individual Prayer Controls**: Toggle reminders independently for Fajr, Sunrise, Dhuhr, Asr, Maghrib, and Isha.
- **Audio Options**:
  - Full & Short Adhan
  - Fajr Specific Adhan
  - Soft Chimes & Beeps
  - Silent Notifications
- **Background Scheduling**: Works even when the application is closed or your device reboots.

---

## 3. Location & Timezone Options

- **Automatic GPS Location**: Detects your location and timezone automatically.
- **Manual Adjustments**: Custom minute offsets for each prayer time to align perfectly with your local mosque schedule.

---

## 4. Privacy & Offline First Architecture

- **Zero Cloud Tracking**: Light does not transmit location data, IP addresses, or personal information to external servers.
- **100% Offline Support**: Prayer calculations use pure astronomical equations calculated locally on your phone.
